What’s The Real Purpose Of A Resume?

Resume provides your potential employer an overall description of your experience and past skills. It effectively helps you to land a job.

What’s The Worst Resume Mistake Ever?

Try to be to the point. Don’t be too redundant with your words, and try not to mention unnecessary details. The worst mistake possible would be a spelling, vocabulary or grammatical mistake.

What are “Keywords” And How Do I Use Them In My Resume?

Resume keywords are words or phrases that describe specific job requirements. They are the abilities, skills, expertise, and values that the recruiter is looking for in a candidate. Use these words effectively to improve your resume.

My Resume Is Too Long And Wordy. How Do I Cut It Down Efficiently?

If you try to stuff too much info onto your resume, it will get cluttered and lengthy. Trimming your resume means cutting out the excess wording and unimportant information.
